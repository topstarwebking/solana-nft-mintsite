About

Wrath of Odin: NFT Wars

The world of Norse mythology is incredibly rich and profound, giving the Wrath Of Odin Development Team a huge amount of scope and leeway with which to work. From exploring myths and legends to beasts, Gods and famed warriors, we aim to transcend the conventional limitations of time and to bring the past back to life, expressed through the modern lens of NFTs and Blockchain technology. From Odin to The Giants, you’ll find them living here on the Binance Smart Chain and for our other Spin-Offs, on Solana.

Features

Highest Quality NFTs

All NFTs are fully vetted by the Development Team, ensuring only the highest levels of quality and pixel density are produced under the Wrath Of Odin name. Investors gain a piece of art, not just an NFT.

Community driven

High levels of community engagement sustained through tiered, token permission group chats provide investors with a way to contribute their ideas to the project and to interact with eachother. Tiers correlate to amount of $ODIN token owned.

Clear Roadmap

Wrath Of Odin is a long term project that abides faithfully to the official roadmap. Whilst we listen to new community suggestions and incorporate them in the project, we have a clear overall vision for the venture's direction.

Fully Doxxed Team

The Development Team is fully doxxed, meaning investors can have full confidence in being a part of the project; aside from regular AMAs and Medium articles, the team are making a fully transparent and regulated corporation to instil ultimate confidence in the community.

Interoperability

By constructing an interoperability bridge from the Binance Smart Chain across to the world of Ethereum, we aim to increase tradability, functionality and project accessibility across multiple blockchains

Dedicated Marketing

We employ dedicated marketing strategies that give Wrath Of Odin maximum public visibility and room for growth. Concepts include influencer partnerships, a new podcast, bespoke NFTs, SEO and native advertisements!
